## Hybrid Prompt Moves

- Rap plus rock: specify beat source first, then guitar role: `melodic rap over distorted pop-punk guitars`.
- Metal plus electronic: put sound design second: `industrial metal, mechanical synth bass, pounding drums`.
- Soft rock ballad: lead with vocal and emotion: `warm male vocal, soft rock ballad, nostalgic guitar tones`.
- Shoegaze/dream pop: avoid too many rhythmic tags; use texture words like `hazy`, `washed`, `wide`, `reverb`.
- Drill/trap: name 808 behavior if important: `sliding 808s`, `distorted 808s`, or `sub-heavy 808`.
